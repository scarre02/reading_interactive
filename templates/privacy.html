Privacy Policy for Reading Interactive

Last updated: March 29, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Reading Interactive ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our website.

1. Who We Are

Reading Interactive is a non-commercial website designed to offer free and safe reading experiences for children. We are based in the United Kingdom.

2. Our Commitment to Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect, store, or share personal data from children under the age of 13.

Children can access our stories without creating an account or providing any personal information. All features are designed to function anonymously.

If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal data without parental consent, we will delete that data immediately. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has submitted personal information, please contact us.

3. What Information We Collect

We collect minimal data, and only from adult users who voluntarily provide it when signing up:

  • Name (if entered)
  • Email address
  • Date of birth (used to verify age eligibility)

This information is used solely to create and manage user access.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only to:

  • Allow access to premium or saved features (if applicable)
  • Respond to support queries
  • Comply with age verification obligations

5. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use tracking cookies or analytics tools that collect personal data. Any cookies used are essential for site functionality and do not identify users.

6. Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal data only:

  • With your consent
  • When necessary to comply with legal obligations

7. How We Store and Protect Your Data

All data is stored securely and is accessible only by authorised personnel. We implement technical and organisational measures to prevent unauthorised access or misuse.

9. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties. We do not sell or monetise user data.

10. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party voice or text-to-speech APIs. These services do not collect personal data and are used solely to enhance the reading experience.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the law or our services. If we make significant changes, we will post a notice on the website.
